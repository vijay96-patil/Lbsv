About Lal bahadur shastri vidyalaya, Nila

Empowering Rural India with Quality Education and Strong Values

Our Foundation

Our Vision

To be a beacon of learning that nurtures every child with holistic education, instilling strong moral values and a passion for lifelong learning.

Our Mission

  • Provide accessible and quality education to all.
  • Foster creativity through cultural and co-curricular activities.
  • Promote digital literacy with modern teaching methods.
  • Build a safe, encouraging, and inclusive environment.

A Word from Our Principal

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. At ZP School, we are committed to building a nurturing environment where every child is given the opportunity to shine and grow into a responsible, compassionate, and confident individual." - Mr. Vilas Bhojane
